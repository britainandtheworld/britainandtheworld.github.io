NU Britain and the World Scavenger Hunt

This is the online St Katharine Docks Scavenger Hunt for Britain and the World students. Please start the game below.

Task

Find the answers to the following questions, and upload them to the relevant assignment on Canvas.

  1. Who has a plaque on the Dockmaster's house and why? What significant event does it commemorate?
  2. Name two types of goods that were traded in St Katherine's docks.
  3. Who or what is 'Old Kate'?
  4. What caused the fire which demolished the Docks?
  5. What was the name of the pub that stood at the location of the Campus?
  6. Why is a street named after Thomas More on the Eastern side of the Docks? Do you remeber what his most famous writing is called?
